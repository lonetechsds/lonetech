What's Inside

Everything You Need, in One System

From booking to payroll to analytics — explore the modules that power your spa operations.

📅
Smart Reservations
A full-featured calendar that shows all your bookings at a glance. Drag-and-drop to reschedule, click to edit, and never double-book a therapist again.
  • Interactive monthly calendar with color-coded statuses
  • Automatic therapist conflict detection
  • Drag-and-drop rescheduling
  • Daily schedule view with timeline
  • Walk-in, online booking, and home service support
📱
Mobile-Optimized View
Your receptionist or manager can manage bookings right from their phone. The mobile view is designed for quick actions — swipe between days, tap to confirm or reschedule.
  • Day-by-day card view optimized for small screens
  • One-tap status updates (confirm, cancel, done)
  • Real-time sync with desktop version
  • Quick-add new bookings on the go
  • Works offline — syncs when back online
💰
Automated Payroll
Stop spending hours on spreadsheets. The system computes commissions, overtime, bonuses, and deductions for every therapist — automatically, every cut-off.
  • Per-service commission calculation
  • Daily hour bonus tiers (4h → ₱80 up to 7h+ → ₱160)
  • Overtime with GC early arrival tracking
  • Attendance bonus & rest day work pay
  • Digital payslips — printable or screenshot-ready
📊
Break-even & Profitability
Know exactly how much revenue you need each month to cover all expenses. Track net profit, margins, and trends — with visual charts that make the data easy to understand.
  • Monthly & quarterly financial dashboards
  • Revenue vs. cost trend lines
  • Expense tracking (rent, utilities, supplies)
  • P&L statement generation
  • AI-powered insights & forecasting
📈
Business Analytics
Make data-driven decisions. See which services are most popular, which therapists are most productive, and how your revenue trends over time — all in real-time dashboards.
  • Revenue, bookings, and tips tracking
  • Top services by frequency and revenue
  • Therapist performance leaderboards
  • Payment mode breakdown (cash, GCash, split)
  • Trend analysis with 7-day to yearly ranges
⏰
Time Clock & Attendance
No more paper time logs. Therapists clock in with a tap — with optional geofencing to verify they're actually at the spa. Late arrivals and overtime are tracked automatically.
  • One-tap clock in / clock out
  • GPS geofencing verification
  • Automatic late & undertime detection
  • Personal view for each therapist
  • Full time log history for payroll
🛠️
Admin Panel
Complete control over your spa configuration. Manage therapists, services, pricing, promos, and payroll settings — all from one centralized dashboard with full audit trail.
  • Therapist management with roles & rest days
  • Service & pricing configuration
  • Member registration & promo management
  • Full audit log — who changed what and when
  • Payroll settings & shift configuration
