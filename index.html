About Us

We Build Software
That Works for You

Lonetech Software & Development Services is a Philippines-based technology company founded by Gaucho Agregado. We specialize in building practical, industry-specific management solutions that automate the tedious parts of running a business — so owners can focus on growing their brand.

Our approach: we work directly with business owners to understand their actual day-to-day operations, then build tools that fit like a glove — not generic software that requires workarounds.

Industry-Specific

Built around how Philippine businesses actually operate, not a one-size-fits-all template.

Fast & Lightweight

No heavy apps to install. Works in any browser, on any device — even offline.

Secure & Reliable

Role-based access, full audit trails, and cloud-backed data with automatic sync.

Hands-on Support

Direct access to the developer. Fast responses, real solutions — not a support ticket queue.

Our Vision

To empower every Filipino service business with simple, affordable technology

So owners can focus on growing their business and serving their customers — not drowning in paperwork, spreadsheets, and manual processes.

Our Mission

We build easy-to-use management systems that automate daily operations

Starting with the wellness industry and expanding to every service business in the Philippines where owners are still doing everything by hand.
